---
name: MPU6050
description: 6-axis motion tracking device with a 3-axis gyroscope and a 3-axis accelerometer.
---

## Operation

- AD0 is tied to VCC to change MPU6050's I2C address to '0x69', always read from this address.

- Always convert raw MPU6050 readings using correct sensitivity (LSB/g, LSB/dps) to get values in g and deg/s.

- Normalize accel and gyro separately by their configured ranges (e.g., ±2g, ±250 dps) to map values into ~[-1, 1].

- Unless mentioned otherwise, display/print in format of "A[g]: X=+0.0 Y=-0.1 Z=+1.0" and "G[d/s]: X=+1.2 Y=-0.4 Z=+0.0"
